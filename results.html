Standout moments

Three performances
that defined the season.

Extracting a leg win from total chaos. Back-to-back wins in Indore. Finishing a broken car to seal the title.

Indore · Jan '26

Won both legs back-to-back. Most dominant single-round performance of the season. On a different line than everyone else.

Bengaluru K1000

First leg win in chaotic conditions. "Bangalore K1000 was 100% chaos." Stages changing minute to minute — and still the fastest man.

Nashik · Grit

Bent rear beam. DNF Leg 1. Came back P3 Leg 2 — sealed the runner-up title anyway. The story that sells: intelligent recovery under pressure.

The hardware

Snap Racing swept the board.

Debut-season silverware: 1st Runner-Up JINRC (both legs) and P4 INRC3 overall. Two drivers from Calicut, one team, and a season that told everyone Snap Racing had arrived.
