The Duo

The short version

Making a mark in Indian motorsport.

Rally is India's fastest-growing motorsport. This is the crew writing its next chapter — from a city that's never had one.

Abhimanyu, 27, from Calicut. IIT Bombay Mechanical Engineering '17. Finance professional in Bengaluru. A karting session in June 2024 turned into a national rally seat thirteen months later — and a runner-up trophy five months after that.

Vijay Anand, co-driver, also from Calicut. The reason #41 takes the right line at 140 km/h in the blind. Engineer on the intercom, friend in the seat.

"After the first jump in my car, I knew this was my place."
