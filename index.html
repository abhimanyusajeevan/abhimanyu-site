The Driver

NFS to national podium.

NRI kid → PlayStation petrolhead → IIT Bombay mechanical engineer → Bengaluru finance professional → JINRC Runner-Up in his debut season. The story already writes itself.

Abhimanyu, 27, from Kozhikode, Kerala, is one of Indian rally's most compelling stories. He grew up in Dubai, returned to India in the eighth standard, and spent years with racing at the back of his mind. A karting session in Bengaluru in June 2024 changed everything.

He caught the eye of national rally driver Athira Murali, joined Garage Snap Racing, and — within a single season — was trading times with India's most experienced rally drivers. He's already carrying confirmed personal sponsors into 2026–27.

"After the first jump in my car, I knew this was my place."
