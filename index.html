Abhimanyu Sajeevan is an Indian national rally driver competing under the Blueband Sports banner in a VW Polo. A Mechanical Engineering graduate from IIT Bombay (Class of 2017), he brings the same precision he applies on gravel stages to his role as a Finance & Analytics Lead.

His 2026 season culminated in a JNRC Runner-Up finish — a national podium earned through relentless preparation, smart pace notes and MRF rubber pushed to its limits.
